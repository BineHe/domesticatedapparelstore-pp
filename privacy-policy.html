Privacy Policy

MillennialBestiesPinHelper — Pinterest API integration

Last updated: 2026-03-06 Contact: a.andbeyond.print@gmail.com
This page is provided to meet Pinterest API requirements.

1) Who this App is for

MillennialBestiesPinHelper (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a tool to help organize and prepare Pinterest content (for example, drafting pin text, managing media assets, and assisting with publishing workflows). The App is primarily intended for use by the account owner.

2) Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information:

A. Pinterest account data (via Pinterest API)

  • Basic account and profile information that the Pinterest API makes available to authorized apps (e.g., account identifiers).
  • Content-related data required for the App’s features, such as boards, pins, pin metadata, and publishing status — only to the extent permitted by the Pinterest API scopes you authorize.

B. Content you provide

  • Pin titles, descriptions, keywords, hashtags, and other text you enter or generate using the App.
  • Media files you select for uploads (e.g., images/videos) and related file metadata (e.g., filenames).

C. Authentication data

  • OAuth tokens (e.g., access tokens/refresh tokens) required to connect to Pinterest.
  • Tokens are used solely to authenticate requests to Pinterest on your behalf.

D. Technical data (minimal)

  • Basic logs necessary to diagnose errors and keep the App reliable (e.g., timestamps, error codes).
  • We do not intentionally collect precise location data.

3) How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Connect to Pinterest and perform actions you request (e.g., create or update pins, read board information).
  • Generate or improve drafts you create in the App (e.g., suggested pin copy).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations if required.

4) What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not share your Pinterest data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5) Sharing of information

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Pinterest: The App interacts with Pinterest through the Pinterest API to perform requested actions.
  • Service providers (if applicable): If the App is hosted using third-party infrastructure (e.g., hosting provider, cloud storage), those providers may process limited data as needed to operate the service. They are required to protect the data and use it only to provide services to us.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

6) Data storage and retention

  • The App retains information only for as long as necessary to provide its functionality.
  • Pinterest OAuth tokens may be stored to keep the connection active until you disconnect the App or revoke access.
  • Draft content and logs may be retained to support your workflow and to debug issues.
  • You can request deletion of stored data (see Section 9).

7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including restricting access and using secure methods for storing credentials (where supported). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we aim to protect your information appropriately.

8) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Your choices and rights

  • Disconnect the App: You can revoke the App’s access in your Pinterest account settings at any time.
  • Request deletion: You may request deletion of data the App stores (if any) by contacting us (see Section 11).
  • Update/correct content: You can edit content you created in the App directly.

10) International transfers

If you access the App from outside the country where the App is operated/hosted, your information may be transferred and processed in other countries. We take reasonable steps to protect information in line with this Policy.
