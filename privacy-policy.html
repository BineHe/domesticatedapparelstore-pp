Privacy Policy

MillennialBestiesPinHelper — Pinterest API integration

Last updated: 2026-03-09 Contact: a.andbeyond.print@gmail.com
This policy is provided to meet Pinterest API requirements and explain exactly what this app does and does not do.

1) Who this App is for

MillennialBestiesPinHelper (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is an internal workflow tool for the owner of MillenialBestieCo to prepare and publish Pinterest content for the owner’s own business account.

The App is not a public multi-tenant SaaS and is not intended to post for unrelated third-party client accounts.

2) Information we process

Depending on usage and granted scopes, we may process the following categories:

A. Pinterest account and content data (via Pinterest API)

  • Basic account identifiers and account metadata provided by Pinterest API.
  • Board and pin metadata required to support read/write features you explicitly authorize.
  • Publish status data (e.g., success/failure, returned pin IDs) for reliability and auditability.

B. Content you provide

  • Pin titles, descriptions, keyword focus, hashtags, and workflow notes.
  • Media files and file references you upload/select for publishing.

C. Authentication data

  • OAuth credentials/tokens required to connect to Pinterest APIs.
  • Tokens are used solely to authenticate API requests on your behalf.

D. Technical/operational logs (minimal)

  • Timestamps, job states, and error diagnostics required to keep the service stable and secure.
  • We do not intentionally collect precise location data.

3) How we use information

  • Connect your Pinterest account and execute actions you explicitly request.
  • Prepare draft content and run validation (e.g., required fields, duplicate checks).
  • Publish content only after manual user approval.
  • Maintain reliability, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable.

4) What we do not do

  • We do not auto-post without human approval.
  • We do not run uncontrolled bulk posting or spam workflows.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use data for third-party advertising resale or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not share Pinterest data with third parties for their own marketing.
  • We do not knowingly upload content you do not own or license.

5) Sharing of information

We share data only in limited cases:

  • Pinterest: to perform requested API operations.
  • Service providers (if applicable): hosting/storage/logging vendors acting under confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
  • Legal requirements: where disclosure is required by law or valid legal process.

6) Legal basis (where GDPR applies)

  • Performance of a service/contract: to provide requested app functionality.
  • Legitimate interests: security, abuse prevention, reliability, and diagnostics.
  • Legal obligation: compliance duties when applicable.
  • Consent: where legally required (including API authorization flow).

7) Data storage and retention

  • We retain data only as long as required for operational purposes.
  • OAuth tokens are kept only while needed for account connection and are removable by revocation/disconnect.
  • Drafts and logs may be retained for workflow continuity and debugging.
  • You can request deletion of stored app data (see Section 11).

8) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access restrictions, secure secret handling, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve controls to reduce risk.

9) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) International transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we apply reasonable safeguards appropriate to the transfer and applicable law.

11) Your choices and rights

  • Disconnect the App: revoke access in Pinterest account settings at any time.
  • Request deletion: request deletion of locally stored data by contacting us.
  • Update/correct data: edit your own draft content directly in the app workflow.
